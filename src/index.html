Ballroom Dance Lessons for Singles and Couples

Adult dance classes for beginners

Welcome to Ann Arbor’s Premier Ballroom Dance Studio, Arthur Murray Dance Studio Ann Arbor. We specialize in ballroom dance classes for adults, couples, and beginners right here in Ann Arbor. Whether you're preparing for your wedding dance, want to enjoy fun social dance lessons, or prefer private ballroom dance lessons, our experienced instructors make it fun, quick, and easy. Join us today and discover the excitement of partner dancing in a welcoming, supportive environment.

We teach any dance you can dance with a partner: Salsa, Swing, Tango, or Country? Latin, Ballroom, 2-step? No Problem! With over 30 styles of dance to choose from, we've got you covered.

No partner? No experience? No problem.

Whether you’re looking for a crash course or want to be the next contestant on Dancing with the Stars, we'll personalize a program just for you. Walk in, dance out!

Arthur Murray—The Legacy

Arthur Murray, our founder, believed that EVERYONE can learn to dance. What started as mail order footprints, quickly grew into a family of franchised dance studios with over 300 locations worldwide. Well over a century later, we continue his tradition of innovative dance teaching. Whether you’ve been dancing for years or have two left feet, our fool-proof method is designed to get you on the dance floor fast!
